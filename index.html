About Us

We run Magic: The Gathering tournaments around Darwin with help from Comics NT and Darwin Tabletop Gamers Association.

Events & Pricing

Standard - $15

  • 3 rounds
  • 1 booster per round win
  • Plus 1 booster for 0-3
  • Plus 1 booster for 1st, 2nd
  • Plus 1 booster for 3rd with 8 or more players
  • Plus showdown boosters or FNM cards

Draft - $25 ($50 for Masters)

  • 3 rounds
  • 1 standard booster per round win
  • Plus 1 booster for 0-3
  • Plus event promo cards depending on event

Sealed - $45

  • 6 standard booster sealed deck
  • 3 rounds
  • 1 standard booster per round win
  • Plus 1 booster for 0-3
  • Plus event promo cards depending on event

Modern - $10

  • 8 players = 3 rounds
  • 9 to 16 players = 4 rounds
  • Modern card prizes for 1st, 2nd, 3rd
  • Door prize with 12 or more players
